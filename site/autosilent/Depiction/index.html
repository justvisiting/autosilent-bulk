Auto Silent
Enjoy the silence!
You should only upgrade from 3.6,3.5 to 3.9 if you are seeing any issue with the app.
  • Do you ever forget to put your iPhone in silent mode?
  • Do you ever get disturbed by that iPhone alert?
  • Ever missed that important call because you forgot to turn off that silent switch?
  • Is your iPhone Silent Switch broken or loose?
  • App puts your iPhone into silent mode automatically, when it's important.
  • App turns on sounds automatically once the meeting or "Do not disturb" time is over.
  • App can Bypass/Disable iPhone Silent Switch - Get sounds back.

Features

  • No need to remember to put phone into silent mode
  • Phone goes into silent mode automatically during meetings and in night
  • Sound settings automatically gets restored once meeting is over or when you wake up in the morning
  • Only settings configured by you are applied. e.g: Alarm stays ON even in silent mode
  • Runs in background, no need to keep app open
  • Enjoy the sleep without any interruption
  • Attend meetings without getting embarrassed

The app can be downloaded from Cydia, search for "Auto Silent"

Enhancements in 3.0.0 release

  • Ability to create custom schedule and profiles.
  • Ability to assign different profiles to different schedules.
  • Added app status icon on Status bar.

Enhancements in 2.0.0 release

  • Added Manual mode, you can put phone into silent mode right away.
  • Now you can Fix/ByPass iPhone Silent Switch.
  • Reduced battery consumption to almost negligible.
  • With Manual mode, there is timer. iPhone will come out of silent mode automatically once timer goes off
  • Complete application redsign

Enhancements in 1.5.2 release

  • Now Keyboard Sound and Lock Sounds can also be configured
  • No need to wait for settings to be applied. Any change in setting is applied instantly
  • Phone goes in silent mode only for the meetings with Availability set to "Busy"
  • Moved current phone status indictor to the main page
  • In silent mode, now Ringer can be set to silent for all, including contacts with special ringtones
