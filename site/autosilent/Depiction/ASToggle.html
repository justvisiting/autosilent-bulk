Auto Silent
Enjoy the silence!

Auto Silent SBSettings Toggle

  • SBSettings shortcut to silent and unsilent phone
  • The timer to is set to 7 days when this toggle is used. Timer can be changed by going to Auto Silent app
  • Requires Auto Silent app.

Auto Silent app features

  • Do you ever forget to put your iPhone in silent mode?
  • Do you ever get disturbed by that iPhone alert?
  • Ever missed that important call because you forgot to turn off that silent switch?
  • Is your iPhone Silent Switch broken or loose?
  • App puts your iPhone into silent mode automatically, when it's important.
  • App turns on sounds automatically once the meeting or "Do not disturb" time is over.
  • App can Bypass/Disable iPhone Silent Switch - Get sounds back.

Features

  • No need to remember to put phone into silent mode
  • Phone goes into silent mode automatically during meetings and in night
  • Sound settings automatically gets restored once meeting is over or when you wake up in the morning
  • Only settings configured by you are applied. e.g: Alarm stays ON even in silent mode
  • Runs in background, no need to keep app open
  • Enjoy the sleep without any interruption
  • Attend meetings without getting embarrassed

The app can be downloaded from Cydia, search for "Auto Silent"
