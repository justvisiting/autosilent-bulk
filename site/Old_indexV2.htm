Quick Look at what "Auto Silent" app can do for you

  • Automatically syncs with your iPhone Calendar and puts iPhone into silent mode during meetings.
  • You can configure "do not disturb" time and app will put iPhone into silent mode during that time. 
  • Unlike "Silent switch" where all sounds go off, you can keep sounds ON which you like. E.g: You can keep ringtone ON but SMS alert OFF.

  • Reverts back to original sound settings once meeting or "do not disturb" time is over.
