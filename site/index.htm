The best sound management experience

Enjoy automatic enabling and disabling of your iPhone sounds based on your schedule! Let no alerts disturb you when it matters the most to you.

With manual mode you can disable sounds or vibration anytime and set a time to automatically enable them even after you exit the app. Now you can safely forget to turn on the sounds after that exciting movie you just watched!

Auto Silent will automatically put your iPhone in silent mode according to schedule defined by you or meetings. Once the event is over, it will bring the phone back out of silent mode.

Now, you can always enjoy your quiet hours without getting disturbed!
