App Can be downloaded from Cydia

New 1.5.4 version is released and available to download. We highly recommend upgrading to this version. It can be downloaded as described below:
  1. Go to Cydia (Need to jailbreak, it is easy take few steps. Read Myths about jailbreaking)
  2. Click on Manage at the bottom
  3. Click on Sources
  4. Click on add at top right
  5. In Url box, enter http://iphonepackers.info/app/repo
  6. Click "Add Source" and "Done" at top right
  7. Click on search & search for "Auto Silent".
